Shovel(2020)

Exercise of design for the human body.
The brief was to identify ergonomic risk in home gardening and redesign a garden tool.
Study and validation on grip, length and use of a shovel based on anthropometric data.


See the project file ->

Newton Scale(2021)

Example of DFM.
An exercise of different manufacturing technologies and finite element method to redesign the newton scale components.

See the project page ->

Prima chair colection(2022)

Furniture design, from research to prototype.
The brief was to design a ready-to-assemble indoor chair for online and retail commercialization, using local industries.
‍
Around 300 hours of work for a 7-chair collection inspired by the concept ‘what if a chair would be made by Aston Martin.’

See the project page ->
